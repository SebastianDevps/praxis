## The Single Message Rule

One ad = one idea. The viewer decides in under 2 seconds whether to stop scrolling. Multiple messages means none land.

Before designing, write the one sentence the ad must communicate. Everything else is removed.

## Hook in the First Beat

- Static: the hook is the visual + headline together in the first glance
- Video: the hook lands in the first 1–3 seconds, before the viewer can skip
- Hook strategies: unexpected visual, a number that surprises, a question the viewer is already asking

## Format Fit per Platform

| Platform | Dominant format | Key constraint |
|----------|----------------|---------------|
| Instagram Feed | 1:1, 4:5 | No critical content in bottom 20% (UI overlap) |
| Instagram Story / Reels | 9:16 | Safe zone: center 60% of height |
| Meta Feed | 1.91:1 | Short copy; image carries the message |
| LinkedIn Feed | 1.91:1 or 1:1 | More copy tolerance; professional tone |
| Display banner | 300×250, 728×90, 160×600 | Logo + CTA legible at small size |

Design for the tightest constraint first, then adapt upward.

## CTA

- Verb-first, specific: "Start free trial" not "Learn more"
- One CTA per creative — two CTAs split attention
- Contrast ratio ≥ 4.5:1 against the button background (see `a11y-baseline`)

## Brand Safety

- Use only licensed or brand-owned assets
- Check overlay text contrast on every image variant — photos change
- Match the brand's voice; do not invent a new tone for the ad channel
